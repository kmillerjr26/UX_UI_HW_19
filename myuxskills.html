My Skills

User Research

I create a research plan including in-person interview questions and surveys to test my hypothesis about the user’s needs. These answers serve as the basis for defining the problem I need to solve.

User Empathy

I seek to uncover the user’s needs, motivations, and pain points by asking them questions and synthesizing their answers through affinity diagrams and persona creation.

Qualitative Interviews

Using a hypothesis of the user and a well-informed knowledge base of the topic at hand, I ask the user open-ended questions about their past experiences and what they wish to see in the future.
